THE VARIOUS ORAGAMI DESIGNS ARE:-

CAMEL

1] Camel's ears are furry
2] Camel can move easily across the sand
because of its specially designed feet
3] When they find water, they will drink
as much as possible

CHAMELEON

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are
    part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin colour is an important part
    of communication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that
    they use to wrap around tree branches

BEAR

  1. Brown bears in the wild are mainly active
    at dusk and at night.
  2. The brown bear can reach a weight of
    between 150 and 370 kilogrammes depending
    on age, sex and season
  3. Despite their weight, the animals can cover
    short distances at speeds of up to 50 km/h
